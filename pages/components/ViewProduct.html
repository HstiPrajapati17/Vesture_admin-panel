Product overview

The Classic Linen Shirt is designed for breathability and ease during warm-weather wear. Cut in a classic, relaxed silhouette with reinforced seams on high-stress areas, this piece balances casual comfort with refined finishings (mother-of-pearl buttons, single-needle hems).

Fabric & construction
  • Yarn & weave: 100% long-staple linen, plain weave, pre-washed to reduce shrinkage.
  • Gram weight: ~210 g/m² providing a medium-light hand and durable drape.
  • Finish: Softening wash (eco-friendly), enzyme-treated for pilling resistance.
Sustainability & compliance

Sourced under our vendor charter. Fabrics carry Oeko-Tex® standard confirmation (supplier certificate on file). Packaging uses recycled paperboard and compostable polybags.

Selling points & merchandising notes
  • Key seasonal hero — highlight in warm-weather campaigns for its breathability and natural fibers.
  • Pair with light chinos or premium denim; recommend 'S'–'L' for standard fit charts.
